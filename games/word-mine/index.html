Word Mine

Dig out a hidden word one letter at a time.

  • 1Tap a letter. Every matching position is revealed.
  • 2A correct letter keeps the turn. A wrong letter costs a life and passes the turn in two-player mode.
  • 3In two-player mode, whoever uncovers the final hidden letter wins the round. First to 3 rounds wins.
  • 4In solo mode, reveal the whole word before all six lives are gone.
