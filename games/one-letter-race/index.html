One-Letter Race

Turn the start word into the goal word by changing one letter at a time.

  • 1The start word, goal word, and every step must be legal words of the same length. Start and goal words come from a curated common-word puzzle list.
  • 2Each new word must differ from the previous word by exactly one letter.
  • 3Every win makes the next puzzle one letter longer, starting at 3 letters and building up to 8.
  • 4In 2-player mode, each player gets the same real-word puzzle and 45 seconds. Fewer moves wins; time breaks ties.
