How to play

Protect your cross.
Break theirs.

Shape Siege is a tactical duel on a 9×9 board. Each side starts with nine circles in front of five stronger pieces, with empty gaps between them. Player 2 begins at the top; Player 1 begins at the bottom. The starting player is chosen at random.

○Circle

Move 1 or 2 squares straight forward or backward. Capture exactly 1 square diagonally forward. Circles never jump.

△Triangle

Move 1 or 2 squares in the direction it points, then use the small on-board control to keep facing or turn 90° left or right. It may also turn in place. An adjacent enemy in its final direction is captured automatically.

□Square

Move any distance horizontally or vertically. It may jump over one enemy, which survives, then land on an empty square or capture the next enemy. Friendly pieces and a second obstacle block it.

×Cross

Move up to 3 squares in any straight or diagonal direction. It captures every enemy crossed along the route. Friendly pieces stop it.

Check rules: you may not finish a move with your own cross threatened. Checkmate wins. Stalemate, a threefold repetition, or 60 consecutive player moves without a capture ends the game in a draw.
