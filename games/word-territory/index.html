Word Territory

Build words through connected tiles and lock down the board.

  • 1Select letters that touch horizontally or vertically. Diagonal moves and reused tiles are not allowed.
  • 2Words must contain at least 3 letters and appear in the built-in dictionary.
  • 3Neutral tiles in a legal word become your colour. You may reuse your own tiles, but your opponent can never use them.
  • 4Each owned tile is worth 1 point. Every turn lasts 10 seconds.
  • 5Two consecutive passes or timeouts—one turn after another—end the game. The most territory wins.
Board size

Boards are generated with extra vowels and common letters, then checked to make sure plenty of legal words are available.
