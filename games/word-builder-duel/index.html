Word Builder Duel

Grow one shared word by adding letters to either end.

  • 1On your turn, choose left or right and add exactly one letter.
  • 2The growing letters must still appear together inside at least one legal word.
  • 3When the letters form a legal word of 3 or more letters, you may bank it for points equal to its length.
  • 4An impossible move gives the opponent 2 points. First to 20 wins.
